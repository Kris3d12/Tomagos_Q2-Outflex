About Me: Story Of My Life

The Idlers and the Fools I am not a typical child. I was born 2 months earlier than my supposed birth month. Born on March 5th 2011 through cesarean section in a catholic hospital in Santa Mesa Manila. Though I was a preemie baby, I had a strong bones and a very healthy physique, The pediatrician told my parents that I could be either extremely smart or I could be part of the abnormal spectrum. So the doctor's decide to place me in an incubator for a month under observation and considered to be in intensive care.

My mom never thought less but instead she trust God with his will and prayed that I will grow fast since I was a toothpaste tube size baby too small for nurses and doctors professional observation. But still mom is eager to make me grow normal and strong. In the hospital I was known as the miracle baby after a month of observation. All my lab works came out normal and I was already a little bigger now size of a 1.5 liter pop soda.

And so, at the age of One I can speak the language well. No baby talk, my mom always talk to me in a normal way and so it was easy for me to learn to talk. I was not a sickly baby despite of my being a premature. I am always fascinated on robots and gadgets and also the constellation. Everytime my mom and I goes out of the house and enjoy the open air, she always tell me stories about the constellation and how they symbolize star-crossed lovers, About the gods and goddesses. This inspired me to dream about going to the moon and see how great and wonderful God's creation.
