WEB PAGE 2-HAPPINESS

The Way to Happiness: Keep your heart free from resentment, your mind from fear. Live humbly, expect little, share much. Fill your life with compassion. Scatter warmth. Forget ego, think of others. Do as you would be done by. Try this for a week and you will be surprised. KRISTINA CASSANDRA N. TOMAGOS, BENEVOLENT, G-15
