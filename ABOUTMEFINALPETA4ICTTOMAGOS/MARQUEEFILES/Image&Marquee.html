THE EMBERS OF GRACE

In realms of light, a being so pure,

With wings outstretched, forever sure.

A fallen angel, lost in shade,

A tragic story, light betrayed.

Once graced in radiance, a celestial sight,

Now draped in shadows, cloaked by night.

A creature of beauty, marked by sin,

A fallen angel, lost within.

A symbol of hope, in darkened air,

A reminder of love, beyond compare.

A fallen angel, bound in lore,

A testament to love's core.

Though cast from grace, a heart so true,

A fallen angel, still shining through.

In darkest night, a gentle glow,

A fallen angel, bright below.
